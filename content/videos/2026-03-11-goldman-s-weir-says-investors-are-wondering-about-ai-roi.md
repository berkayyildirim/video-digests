## TLDR

Goldman Sachs believes AI investment concerns are overblown - while questioning ROI is valid, current spending isn't economically imbalanced and historically markets recover from geopolitical shocks within 8 weeks. They expect 10% earnings growth but compressed valuations, and view AI job displacement as manageable given America's job creation engine.
